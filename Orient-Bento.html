Meaning Operating System

A meaning system for humans and machines

Orient is where knowledge becomes shared understanding — captured, resolved, framed, measured and remembered — so people and the agents working alongside them act from the same picture of what's true, decided and ready to move forward.

Two customers of meaning. Humans use it to think. Agents use it to understand.
